Working with the lab

We are always interested in hearing from people from any field that are passionate and open-minded about engineering biological systems. We believe that the most exciting science takes place at the boundaries between disciplines. If this resonates with you then contact us!

Students of the SynBio CDT are welcome to visit the lab directly to see what is going on and discuss potential project ideas in person.
